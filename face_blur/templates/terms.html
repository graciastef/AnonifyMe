Terms & Conditions

Last updated: May 2025

1. Data Storage & Retention

Your uploaded video and any whitelist face images are stored temporarily on our secure servers solely for the purpose of processing. All data is automatically deleted within 2 hours of upload, regardless of whether processing completes successfully.

2. Extended Retention Conditions

In the following circumstances, your data may be retained;

  • Processing fails due to a server error and requires investigation
  • A dispute or support request is raised regarding your session
  • Suspected misuse or abuse of the service is detected
  • One or both Face Detection/Recognition model underperforms on certain frames in the video
  • Whitelisted faces are blurred in some frames of the video

3. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used exclusively for face detection and blurring. We do not share, sell, or use your video content for training AI models or any other purpose.

4. Security

All uploads are transmitted over encrypted connections. Stored files are access-controlled and not publicly accessible.

5. Your Consent

By checking the box and proceeding, you confirm you have the right to process the video content you upload and consent to the temporary storage described above.
